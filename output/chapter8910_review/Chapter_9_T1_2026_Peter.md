Solutions for Externalities
Property Rights 
Suppose the School of Maths  has a right to quiet.
•School of Rock will offer up to $8000  to school of Maths  for permission 
to make noise i.e to buy the ‘silence’ right.  
•School of Maths  would collect the $8000 but would then give up  $14000 
in fees
•School of Maths  therefore declines the offer and enforces its right to 
silence 
•School  Maths  earns $14000 and School of Rock earns $2000. Total profit 
is $16000
(the alternative I.e. noise would have $10000 for school of Rock and Zero 
for School of Maths )
Therefore the efficient result (silence ) is achieved
