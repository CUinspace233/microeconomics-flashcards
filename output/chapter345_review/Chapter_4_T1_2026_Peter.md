Pareto Efficiency

impossible to make any individual better off 
without making at least one other individual 
worse off.

The Invisible Hand Principle: 
The Invisible Hand Principle 
 states that 
individuals’ independent efforts to maximize 
their gains (profits for sellers; utility for buyers) 
will generally be beneficial for society and result 
in the socially optimal allocation of resources.
